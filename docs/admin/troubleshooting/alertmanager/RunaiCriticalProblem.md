## Meaning

One of the critical Run:ai alerts is active.

## Impact

The impact is based on the active alert.

## Severity

Critical

## Diagnosis

Check Run:ai alerts in Prometheus.

## Mitigation

Resolve the active alert. If you cannot, then contact Run:ai.
